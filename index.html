Frequently Asked Questions

  • What time are you avilable for an estimate?

    Estimates can be given at all times during the week that's best for you. We understand how busy working professionals can be and try to be available based on customers' schedules.

  • Is the company licensed and insured/ Who is liable for property damage?

    Marc's Landscaping is licensed and insured. Additionally, we will take full responsibility for accidental damage caused by us (This is rarely an issue but its best to say this to put customers at ease).

  • I dont live in an area listed what should I do?

    Marc's Landscaping tries its best to cover all needs of customers. In the instance that you are outside of our servicing area, you simply need to contact us and we will see if it's possible to extend our range.

  • Do you work with HOA's?

    Yes, we at Marc's Landscaping work with HOA's. all that's needed is clear communication. We do consider all contracts and will do our best to fulfill all requirements an HOA can throw at us.

  • Do you do party decorations ?

    Yes, we do party decorations but we also do lawn service for any special occasions. Just contact us prior so we can negotiate a price and a timeframe.

  • How often do you come for monthly maintance?

    Our monthly maintenance has at least one scheduled visit. The real answer however is based on how multiple different factors so we can not have a single answer for all. With that being said, the best way to know for sure is by contacting us. If you have a preference on when and how often you want us to be there, just let us know and we will adjust accordingly.

  • Whats the best way to contact the company?

    The best way to contact us is whichever way you prefer. we can contact us at all times and we will get back to you as soon as possible.

  • Other...?

    If you have any other questions, please feel free to contact us using your prefered methood of communication.
